Game Rules (1-10)
The Objective
Score more runs than the CPU. You play two innings: one as the Batsman and one as the Bowler.

How to Play
Select a number from 1 to 10.
If your number matches the CPU, the batsman is OUT.
If the numbers are different, the batsman adds their number to the total score.
Winning the Match
Set a Target in the first innings. In the second innings, the bowler must get the batsman out before they cross that target.
